28mm wide-angle shots and 8x optical zooming with a compact body

This compact body is ready to meet a variety of shooting needs with its 28mm wide-angle lens and 8x optical zoom. MEGA O.I.S. also lets you capture images without blurring even when zooming, where hand-shake often occurs.

 

Capture magnificent shots with a single press of the iA button

Just press the iA button, and leave everything else to the camera. In addition to automatically selecting the optimal Scene mode from among six types to match your subject, the camera also automatically corrects blurring, Focussing and brightness problems. This gives you beautiful photos every time, without mistakes.
